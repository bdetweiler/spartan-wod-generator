On the Road Again

Pearls dont lie on the seashore. If you want one, you must dive for it. Chinese proverb

This workout can be done either on the track, or on the road. If you on the road you can use landmarks youve measured out previously or mile markers. Spartan dont make excuses, they find a way.

Warm-up

    Warm up well by jogging/running for 10-15 minutes. You should be sweating before your start this workout.

Main set

    Run 1 mile (high intensity).
    Recover for 1/2 the time it took to run that mile.
    Repeat.

Cool Down:

Easy jog or walk, then stretch.
