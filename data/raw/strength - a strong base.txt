A Strong Base

You cant build a great building on a weak foundation. You must have a solid foundation if youre going to have a strong superstructure. Gordon B. Hinckley

Basic bodyweight exercises need to be mastered before moving on to more difficult lifts and movements. A strong base can be laid with only a few primitive materials.

Warm-up

    Dynamic warm-up
    Jump rope or jog 10 minutes

Main set

    15 burpees
    10 push-ups
    15 walking lunges, each leg
    15 push-ups
    Belly crawl 25 yards x 2
    Bear crawl 25 yards x 2
    15 stone/log/sandbag squats

Repeat 1-4 times.

Run 20 minutes.

Cool Down:

Stretch
