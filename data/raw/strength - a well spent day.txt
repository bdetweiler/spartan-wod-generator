A Well-Spent Day

A well-spent day brings happy sleep. Leonardo da Vinci

Use this workout to allow you to crash into bed exhausted and grinning.

Warm-up

    Jump rope 5-10 minutes

Main set

    10 burpees
    10 push-ups
    5 burpee pull-ups
    3 burpee pull-ups w/knee raises*
    15 crunches
    10 laying leg raises
    5 hanging knee raises

Repeat as needed.

Cool Down:

Stretch
