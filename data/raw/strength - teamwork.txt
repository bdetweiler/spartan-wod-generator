Teamwork

“Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much.” – Helen Keller

Looking for a great workout to do with a team? Here it is. When rucking or carrying objects you can adjust the weight so that a team of individuals with different abilities can stay together.

    Run 2 miles
    30 burpees

    Run 2 miles carrying 1 to 3 heavy objects (sandbags, rocks, logs, etc.)

    30 burpees each
    30 jumping lunges
    30 bodyweight squats
    30 mountain climbers
    3 x 1 minute plank

    Run 2 miles
    30 burpees
