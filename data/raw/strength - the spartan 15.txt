The Spartan 15

“When deeds speak, words are nothing.” – African Proverb

Warm-up

    Dynamic warm up
    Run/cycle/row/jump rope 5 minutes

Main set

    3 x 15 burpees
    3 x 15 bodyweight squats
    3 x 15 jumping lunges
    5 x 15-yard bear crawl, 5 rope climbs or 15 burpee/pull-ups
    Run/cycle/row/jump rope 15 minutes at high intensity
