Medusas Challenge

Knowing is not enough, we must apply. Willing is not enough, we must do. Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

Warm-up

    75 jumping jacks
    Jog 5-10 minutes
    Dynamic warm-up  3 moves at 25 yards

Main set

Preform 1 minute of each exercise, with 10 second rest between

    push-ups
    sit-ups
    pull-ups
    plank
    squat jumps
    jump rope (fast)
    speed skaters
    mountain climbers
    flutter kicks
    arm curls with sandbag/rock/log
    1-minute rest

Repeat entire sequence 1-3 times

Then, run/jog/row/walk 15 minutes

Dont forget to stretch!
