The Spartan 500

Warm-up

    Dynamic warm up
    Run/cycle/row/jump rope 5 minutes

Main set

    50 burpees
    50 squats (or squat tosses with sandbag)
    50 sit-ups
    5-25 pull-ups
    5 rope climbs (or 5-25 chin-ups)
    Run 1 mile for time

Cool down: Stretch

Usually, 1 set is more than enough. But feel free to go for multiple.
