Strength: Pet Rock – Introduction

Warm-up

    Dynamic Warm-Up

Main set

Perform this set 1-4 times:

    100 meter sprint
    10 push-ups
    10 burpees
    5 pull-ups
    30-second sprint
    1-minute rest 

Then take a walk to find a rock & do this set 1-4 times


    15 squats
    lunge walk 50 yards
    10 chest passes
    10 underhand toss
    1 minute rest between sets

Then carry your rock home, if you don’t have one already. It’s your new workout partner.

Don’t forget to stretch!
