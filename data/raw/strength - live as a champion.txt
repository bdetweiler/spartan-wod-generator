Warm-up

    2 minutes of burpees
    800-meter run above lactic threshold*
    Recover 1-2 minutes

Main set

    2 minutes of jumping rope (or rowing)
    800-meter run above lactic threshold*
    Recover 1-2 minutes

*very hard intensity – close to maximal
