Fast Intervals

To be is to do. Immanuel Kant

Warm-up

    Jog or jump rope 5-15 minutes           // NOTE: I went with Jog
    10 burpees

Main set

    Run 1/4 mile
    2 x 5-15 laying or hanging leg raises   // Note: I went with Laying leg raises
    30-second plank
    Recover 30 seconds

    Run 1/4 mile
    2 x 10- 30 mountain climbers
    30-second plank
    Recover 30 seconds

Cool Down:

Stretch

Repeat main set 1-4 times depending on your ability.
