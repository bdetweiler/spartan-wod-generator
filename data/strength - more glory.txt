More Glory

The greater the difficulty the more glory in surmounting it. Skillful pilots gain their reputation from storms and tempests. Epictetus

Maybe you will find this workout challenging? Good. It will mean that much more when you complete it.

Warm-up

    Dynamic Warm Up - 3 movements x 2 at 25 yards each.

Main set

    75 burpees
    30 squat tosses with sandbag
    5-30 pistol squats each leg (or bodyweight squats)
    5-30 pull-ups
    5 rope climbs (or 5 x 25' bear crawls - get low!)

Cool Down:

Stretch
